3. component vs directive
- componenet has life cycle hook managed by angular.
- one component per DOM , but it can have many directive per DOM
- Componenet is used to break down application into smaller component , Directive is use to design reuasble component
- component has view but directive does not.

4.ngOnInit vs contructor
- OnInit is automatically called when all components are initialized , but contructor require
  a declaration to be called. ngOnInit is called right after constructor .
